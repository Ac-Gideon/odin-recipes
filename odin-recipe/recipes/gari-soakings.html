Gari Soakings

Description

Gari Soakings us a popular Ghanaian dessert made with gari(a powdery food material flour made from the tuberous roots if a cassava plant). As the name implies, it is made by soaking the gari in water and milk.

Ingredients

  • Desired amount of Gari
  • Desired amount of Sweetener(sugar,agave,honey,syrup)
  • Desired amount of Milk
  • Desired amount of Roasted Peanuts
  • Desired amount of Water

Preparation

  1. Pour some gari into a bowl.
  2. Add water such tha it rises way above the level of gari in the bowl.
  3. Some people like to sieve the floating particles, while some do not really care. But it is advisable to, as it might help you to get rid of dirt.
  4. After sieving, add more water(preferably cold water) and add your sweetener of choice,milk and roasted peanuts of any desired amount.
  5. Enjoy your gari soakings.
